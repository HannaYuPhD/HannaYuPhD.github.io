AI Literacy and Critical Thinking in Financial Decision-Making (Working Project)

This course activity is designed to strengthen students' critical thinking skills when evaluating AI-generated recommendations for financial decision-making.

Although this project is still in development, the activity will include the following components:

  • Students prompt AI tools to generate responses about credit card use for individuals with different financial needs.
  • Students evaluate the strengths and weaknesses of AI-generated responses by comparing them with information from official credit card company websites.
  • Students engage in peer review, where classmates provide a second perspective by identifying omissions, questioning assumptions, and offering additional recommendations related to credit card use.

This version is intentionally written for teaching statements, course proposals, and course material documentation.
