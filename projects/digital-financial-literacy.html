Digital Financial Literacy

I developed this course activity on digital financial literacy to address the growing need for college students to locate online financial information and critically evaluate its trustworthiness.

Students enrolled in FSOS 1301 (Cash or Credit: You Need to Know) during Summer 2025 searched for online financial resources aligned with their goals. They evaluated each source based on authority, accuracy, objectivity, currency/timeliness, and structure/accessibility/usability.

Student Prompt

  1. Please use reliable sources to search for financial resources that align with your goals, and clearly state which resource(s) you used.
  2. Based on your findings, evaluate each online resource using the rating system below. Explain which resources you selected and why you consider them reliable and trustworthy.
  3. What does the resource recommend you do? Did you learn anything new, find something surprising, or disagree with any part of it? If there are potential barriers to taking these actions, what are they?
