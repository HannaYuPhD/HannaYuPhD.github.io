About

As a passionate social scientist, I am committed to understanding and improving romantic relationships as well as both mental and physical health through better financial management, particularly within family contexts. I thrive on challenging projects that allow me to push boundaries, question non-evidence-based assumptions, and contribute to meaningful advancements in people’s lives and society. With a strong work ethic, I continuously strive to strengthen my research skills and remain at the forefront of the evolving landscape of families and financial well-being. I welcome opportunities to connect and collaborate in shaping a better future through research, education, and community engagement.
